Our Process

How We Work

A simple, transparent four-step process from first conversation to a live website.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a quick 15-minute call (or email, if you prefer) to understand your business, goals, and vision. No technical knowledge required—just tell us what you need.

02

Design & Build

Our team crafts a custom design tailored to your brand. You'll see a live preview before anything is finalized and can request revisions freely.

03

Review & Refine

You review the full site, we collect your feedback, and we polish every detail until you love it. We don't go live until you're completely satisfied.

04

Launch & Maintain

We deploy your site and handle everything—hosting, security, updates, and monthly maintenance—so you can focus entirely on your business.
